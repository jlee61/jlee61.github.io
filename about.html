About Us

Welcome to our webpage about the Top Five Restaurants in downtown Knoxville!
We are freshman at Farragut High School. We hope to provide a helpful site that lists the best places to eat in downtown Knoxville, especially for visitors that have never been here before.
These are our personal top five based on experience. This is because we grew up in Knoxville, so we know downtown very well and can be your guide to this delightful town!
